About Go Away Ads

Go Away Ads is a lightweight ad blocker designed to improve your Chrome browsing experience without consuming excessive memory or CPU resources. It focuses on simplicity, speed, and privacy — no tracking, no unnecessary permissions, and fully open-source on GitHub.
